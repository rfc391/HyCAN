
# HyCAN Project

## Community and Partnerships

HyCAN is proud to collaborate with the **Heartland BioWorks Hub (HBW)**, a Regional Tech Hub established to catalyze innovation and workforce development in biotechnology. The HBW Membership Agreement outlines key initiatives such as:
- **BioTrain**: Equitable workforce development through education and training programs.
- **BioLaunch**: Support for commercialization and scaling of innovative technologies.
- **BioWorks HQ**: Advanced facilities for training and technology demonstration.

Through this partnership, HyCAN aligns with HBW's goals to strengthen U.S. economic prosperity, national security, and leadership in biotechnology.

For more information, visit: [Heartland BioWorks Hub](https://www.heartlandbioworks.com)

## New Features

### Bioinformatics Tools
HyCAN now integrates advanced bioinformatics workflows, enabling researchers to analyze and visualize molecular and genomic data.

Key Features:
- **Sequence Alignment**: Support for BLAST and other alignment tools.
- **Phylogenetic Analysis**: Generate evolutionary trees and molecular comparisons.
- **Genomic Data Visualization**: Visualize genomic data with Circos-style plots.

---

## Contributing to Biotechnology and Sustainability
HyCAN now includes bioinformatics pipelines and biotech tools to support research in hydrogen production and sustainable energy technologies. These enhancements include:
- Molecular docking simulations for catalyst optimization.
- Genomic data analysis to identify microbial pathways for hydrogen production.
- Data integration from bioreactors and lab experiments.
