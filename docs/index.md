# HyCAN

HyCAN is an open-source platform that accelerates clean hydrogen energy research. It combines machine learning, bioinformatics, and advanced materials science to optimize catalysts, simulate hydrogen production, and explore nanomaterials.

## Features

- **Machine Learning**: Utilize advanced algorithms to optimize hydrogen production processes.
- **Bioinformatics**: Analyze biological data to enhance hydrogen production.
- **Materials Science**: Explore and simulate the properties of nanomaterials.
